YCF Saathi Mobile App Privacy Policy

We at Yash Chopra Foundation (Foundation, we, our, us), offer several programs and benefits to the casual workers who work in the film industry in Bombay. In furtherance of one of our activities and programs for the sector, we have the Saathi program, and have enabled a mobile application the YCF Saathi mobile application (ApplicationApp) which hosts, manages and assists the users (you, your) in managing your benefits as a beneficiary. The Application provides easy interface for key benefits provided by Yash Chopra Foundation to members of its Saathi program, and can be downloaded from Google Play. By accessing, downloading, using this Application in any manner, you agree to have read and understood the terms of this Privacy Policy (Policy) and agree to be governed by these terms contained herein. The Policy governs the manner in which we collect, manage, handle, and process your data, during the entire team of your engagement with us, and after, as may be necessary. We update these terms from time to time, and we urge you to review this Policy time and again, before continuing usage of the App. Your continued use of the App, will be treated as you consenting to be governed by the terms of such updated Policy.

What Information We Collect and Why?

  1. Demographic Details – Name, Address, Age, Gender, Location: we need this information to create a profile for you, and then be able to register you as a valid beneficiary under the programs that are offered by us. We also use this information to ensure that the benefits are validly made available to you. This information will also be used to ascertain your eligibility to participate and avail the benefits of the programs offered by the Foundation.
  2. Correspondence Details – E-mail address, mobile number: we need this information to communicate to you the details of the benefits, and we also need this to update you about the status of your benefits, and other necessary notifications related to the programs of the Foundation that you are eligible for.
  3. Financial Details – Bank Account information: we need this information to be able to assess and credit benefits into your registered bank account.
  4. Feedback and surveys – we may conduct surveys and may also request you to share your feedback with us, with respect to our activities. We use this information to streamline the process of our offerings and conclusion of transactions.
  5. Device Information – hardware and software details, including Device type, ID, IP Address, related browser details, URL, time-stamps, and any other identifier which will enable us to triangulate the user’s location and access details. We use this information to ensure that our App is providing optimal experience to you as a user, and also to be able to ensure that our maintenance measures are implemented well.
  6. Identifiers – we may require you to submit official / government identifiers enabling us to verify your profile and for other statutory purposes
  7. Claim Documents – we may require you to submit such documents which will establish your entitlement to the benefits that are offered under the programs of the Foundation, and such documents may carry personal information about you, or that of the dependents.
  8. Any additional information that is necessary for your enrollment into the SAATHI program of the Foundation. We will communicate the requirement for provisioning of such information by you to us, before we collect and process such information.
